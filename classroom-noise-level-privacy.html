Overview

This privacy policy applies to the Classroom Noise Level app published by GerbilBeard Games. It explains how the app uses microphone access, what information may be processed on the device, and what information may be involved if you contact us for support.

Microphone Access

Classroom Noise Level uses the device microphone to measure sound levels in real time so the app can display classroom noise feedback. The app is intended to analyze volume levels only.

Unless the app behavior changes in a future release, audio recordings are not stored, and raw audio is not transmitted to GerbilBeard Games through the app.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store preferences or setup choices locally on your device, such as thresholds, display options, or classroom session settings, so the app works as expected the next time you open it.

Support Information

If you contact GerbilBeard Games for support, you may provide information such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We use that information only to respond to your request and maintain support records as needed.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Classroom Noise Level is not described on this page as using third-party advertising, third-party tracking, or independent analytics services. If you choose to share diagnostics, crash reports, or similar information through Apple, GerbilBeard Games may receive limited technical information to help improve stability and performance.

How Information Is Used

  • To provide the app's live noise level functionality
  • To save settings and preferences on your device
  • To respond to support questions and troubleshoot issues
  • To improve app reliability, performance, and usability
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect rights and safety

Sharing

GerbilBeard Games does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with platform operators or service providers when necessary to distribute the app through Apple, respond to support requests, comply with the law, or protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

Locally stored app preferences remain on your device until you remove them, reset the app, or delete the app. Support communications may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary to address requests, maintain business records, or meet legal obligations.

Your Choices

You can control microphone permission through your Apple device settings. You can also delete the app, remove locally stored data through your device, and choose whether to share diagnostics with Apple.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
