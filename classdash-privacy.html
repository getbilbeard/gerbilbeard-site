Overview

This privacy policy applies to the ClassDash app published by GerbilBeard Games. It explains the categories of information that may be involved when you download, use, or request support for the app.

Information You May Provide

If you contact GerbilBeard Games for support, you may provide information such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We use that information only to respond to your request and maintain support records as needed.

App and Device Information

ClassDash may rely on information stored on your device to operate correctly, such as app preferences, saved settings, or gameplay-related data. If you choose to share crash reports, diagnostics, or analytics through Apple, GerbilBeard Games may receive limited technical information to help improve app stability and performance.

iCloud

ClassDash uses Apple's iCloud services to store or sync app data across your devices when iCloud is enabled for the app. This may include progress, settings, or other in-app data you create while using ClassDash. iCloud data is handled through your Apple account and is subject to Apple's terms, settings, and privacy practices.

How Information Is Used

  • To provide and maintain the ClassDash app
  • To sync eligible app data across devices using iCloud
  • To respond to support questions and troubleshoot issues
  • To improve app reliability, performance, and usability
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect the app and its users

Sharing

GerbilBeard Games does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers or platform operators when necessary to deliver the app, process App Store distribution, provide iCloud syncing through Apple, respond to support requests, comply with the law, or protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

Support communications and related records may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary to address requests, maintain business records, or meet legal obligations. Data stored locally by the app may remain on your device until you delete the app or remove the data. Data synced through iCloud may remain associated with your Apple account until you delete it from the app, disable iCloud for the app, or remove it through Apple's services and settings.

Your Choices

You can control many privacy settings through your Apple device and Apple account, including analytics sharing, permissions, backups, app deletion, and whether ClassDash may use iCloud. You may also contact GerbilBeard Games with privacy-related questions.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
