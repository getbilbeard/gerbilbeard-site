Overview

GerbilBeard Games values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit our website or interact with our content.

Information We Collect

We collect information in limited ways, including:

  • Information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, or message content when you contact us.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to questions, feedback, or support requests.
  • Improve the website experience, performance, and content quality.
  • Monitor for security issues, abuse, or technical problems.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only when required by law or to protect our rights, users, or services.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our site may use essential cookies or local storage for basic functionality. Embedded third-party content may set additional cookies or collect usage data as described by their own privacy policies.

Embedded Content

Some pages include embedded media such as videos. Those services may collect data about your interactions, including playback activity and device information.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or to comply with legal obligations.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can limit the information you provide and use browser settings to manage cookies. If you contact us, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
